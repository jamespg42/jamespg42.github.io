Summer time by the sea is a five-day festival of contemporary performing arts that takes place near La ciotat, France. In addition to contemporary music, the festival hosts dance, comedy, theatre,  and other arts. Leading pop and rock artists have headlined, alongside thousands of others appearing on smaller stages and performance areas. Films and albums recorded at summer time by the sea will been released, and the festival receives extensive television and newspaper coverage. It is  the largest seaside festival in the world, and is  attended  by around 50,000 people , requiring extensive infrastructure in terms of security, transport, water, and electricity supply. The majority of staff are volunteers, helping the festival to raise millions of pounds for good causes.
